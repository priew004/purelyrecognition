Training Session

In this task, you will see images of objects that are partially hidden behind another image. Your goal is to identify the hidden object as accurately as possible.

Here’s how it works:

  1. You’ll see an image with cut-outs (aperture) that hide part of another image.
  2. Your task is to decide what the hidden object is, AND what the object in the apertures is.
  3. You’ll select your answers from a list of options.
